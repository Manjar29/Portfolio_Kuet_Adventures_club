Who We Are

Built for Adventure, Driven by Teamwork

Our Mission

To inspire KUET students to embrace challenge, discover nature, and develop confidence through responsible adventure.

Our Vision

To become Bangladesh's leading university adventure platform with a strong culture of discipline, leadership, and environmental respect.

Safety First

Every trip follows route planning, group coordination, first-aid readiness, and mentor supervision to ensure safe and enjoyable experiences.

What We Do

Core Club Activities

Hill Trekking

Weekend and semester-break trails for endurance, navigation, and team spirit.

Long Cycling

Early morning and inter-city rides to build fitness and resilience.

Camping Nights

Outdoor camps featuring tents, fire setup, and survival basics.

Rescue Workshops

Practical sessions on first aid, knot techniques, and emergency response.
